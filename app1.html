About This App

Mirror your phone to TV effortlessly with Air Play: Cast to TV! This powerful application allows you to stream, share, and connect all your favorite content directly to your television screen with incredible ease.

Whether you want to share photos with family, watch videos on a bigger screen, or present slideshows, Air Play makes it seamless. The app supports all major smart TVs and streaming devices, offering a smooth and lag-free mirroring experience.

What You Can Do:

  • Entertainment: Watch your favorite movies, shows, and YouTube videos on the big screen
  • Photo Sharing: Display photo galleries and slideshows during family gatherings
  • Gaming: Cast mobile games to TV for a better gaming experience
  • Business: Present documents, spreadsheets, and presentations wirelessly
  • Social Media: Share social media content with friends on a larger display

Compatible Devices:

  • Samsung Smart TVs
  • LG Smart TVs
  • Sony Smart TVs
  • Roku devices
  • Amazon Fire TV
  • Chromecast and other casting devices
