About This App

Experience the future of charging with Reverse Charging Wireless! This innovative app allows users to charge their phone wirelessly using reverse charging technology available on modern smartphones.

Turn your device into a wireless charging pad and share battery power with other compatible devices. Perfect for emergency situations or helping friends when they're running low on battery.

How It Works:

  • Step 1: Enable reverse wireless charging in the app settings
  • Step 2: Place the device you want to charge on the back of your phone
  • Step 3: Monitor the charging progress in real-time
  • Step 4: Stop charging when the device has enough power

Use Cases:

  • Emergency Power: Help friends or family when their device battery is critically low
  • Charge Accessories: Power up wireless earbuds, smartwatches, and other small devices
  • Travel Convenience: Reduce the number of charging cables you need to carry
  • Quick Top-Up: Give devices a quick power boost when outlets aren't available

Requirements:

  • Device with reverse wireless charging capability
  • Android 9.0 or higher
  • Target device must support Qi wireless charging
  • Recommended: Battery level above 30% for optimal performance

Safety Features:

  • Automatic temperature monitoring
  • Overheat protection
  • Low battery warning (stops at 20%)
  • Foreign object detection
