About This App

Connect USB endoscope cameras to your Android device and inspect hard-to-reach areas with ease! This specialized app provides full support for USB OTG endoscope cameras, making professional inspections accessible to everyone.

Perfect for automotive repairs, plumbing inspections, HVAC maintenance, or any situation where you need to see into tight spaces. Capture photos and videos directly from your endoscope camera to document your findings.

What You Can Do:

  • Professional Inspections: Examine pipes, drains, engines, and hard-to-reach mechanical parts
  • Home Maintenance: Inspect walls, ceilings, and hidden spaces for damage or pests
  • Automotive Work: Check engine components, transmission, and other vehicle systems
  • Electronic Repairs: Examine circuit boards and internal device components
  • HVAC Service: Inspect ductwork and ventilation systems

Technical Specifications:

  • Compatible with most USB OTG endoscope cameras
  • Supports resolutions up to 1920x1080
  • Photo format: JPEG, PNG
  • Video format: MP4, AVI
  • Minimum Android version: 5.0
